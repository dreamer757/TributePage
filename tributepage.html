Welcome to the Bake Club Family!

  • Background - Born in Ohio, raised in Virginia
  • Education - Attended the French Culinary Institute's Pastry Program in NYC.
  • MilkBar is a chain of dessert and bakery restaurants across the United States that was founded by chef Christina Tosi. Known for her out of the box flavors, her stores sell cereal milk, milkbar pie, birthday cake truffles, layered cakes and much more!
  • Bake Club started out during the 2020 pandemic as a way to connect bakers around the world. Everyday, Tosi would bake live on Instagram to a recipe posted on her website. Bake Club now continues every Monday @ 2pm.
